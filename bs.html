Dehradun

Dehradun or the “Home of the valley”, is a beautiful hill station in Uttarakhand,India which is enclosed by the mighty ranges of Garhwal Himalayas. This capital city is located at an altitude of 1400 feet and offers a great experience to the adventure junkies, couples and families. A home to beautiful natural caves and magnificent waterfalls, this is a relaxing escape for the people planning to get away for the weekend. As the heat is already rising up, take a quick break to this scenic city and dont miss out on the best things by keeping this handy Dehradun travel guide.

Best Time To Visit

The time between March-June is the best to plan a vacation to Dehradun. During this time the city experiences a huge flock of people who visit this place to enjoy the pleasant weather. While the weather is favorable for tourists, there is a scope of exploring the best sights and indulge into some adventurous activities during this time. The temperature reaches to a maximum of 35 degrees celsius and the minimum temperature is 17 degrees.

Travel Tips --How To Reach

By Road There are many private buses which runs between Delhi to Dehradun and takes at least 5-6 hours to reach. Apart from that the travelers can drive their car from Delhi passing through the NH72A, to reach the city.
By Flight The nearest airport to Dehradun is the Jolly Grant airport which is located at a distance of 20 miles from the main city. One can reach the city by hiring a taxi which will cost around 2,000 approximately.
By Train Dehradun is a major station in many train lines and the time may vary according to the origin station.
